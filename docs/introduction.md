# Introduction

Nyxera Nexus is the enterprise integration layer that binds offensive validation execution and telemetry analytics into a governed security evidence workflow.

Nexus is designed for organizations that require:

- Repeatable validation evidence across teams and environments
- Verifiable chain of custody from execution to executive reporting
- Isolation guarantees for multi-tenant and federated operations
- Integration-ready data contracts for SOC, GRC, and engineering platforms
